About Country Quiz

Country Quiz is a geography-based quiz game designed to test and improve your knowledge of the world’s countries through map borders.

Instead of flags or names, players are challenged to recognize countries based solely on their geographical shapes and borders on the map.

How the Game Works

A country is highlighted on the map

You guess which country it is

The correct answer earns points

Incorrect answers cost a life

The game is simple to play, but progressively more challenging as difficulty increases.

Difficulty Levels

Country Quiz features four different difficulty levels to suit players of all skill levels:

Easy – Large and well-known countries

Medium – Moderately recognizable countries

Hard – Smaller or less obvious country borders

Veteran – Designed for geography experts

Life System

The game uses a life (heart) system to manage gameplay sessions:

Each incorrect answer consumes one life

Lives automatically regenerate every day at 12:00 PM

Players can earn 1 extra life by watching a rewarded advertisement

Additional lives can be purchased:

3 lives during gameplay

10-life and 25-life packs

All purchases are optional, and the game can be played without spending money.
