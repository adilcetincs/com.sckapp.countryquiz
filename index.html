About Country Quiz

Country Quiz is a geography-based quiz game designed to test and improve your knowledge of the world’s countries by recognizing them through their map borders.

Instead of flags or names, players are challenged to identify countries based solely on their geographical shapes.

How the Game Works

  • A country is highlighted on the map
  • You guess which country it is
  • Correct answers earn points
  • Incorrect answers cost a life

The game gets progressively more challenging as difficulty increases.

Difficulty Levels

  • Easy – Large and well-known countries
  • Medium – Moderately recognizable countries
  • Hard – Smaller or less obvious country borders
  • Veteran – Designed for geography experts

Life System

  • Each incorrect answer consumes one life
  • Lives automatically regenerate every day at 12:00 PM
  • Players can earn 1 extra life by watching rewarded ads
  • Additional lives can be purchased:
    • 3 lives during gameplay
    • 10-life and 25-life packs

All purchases are optional; the game can be played without spending money.
